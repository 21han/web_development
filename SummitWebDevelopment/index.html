What do we do?

Founded in 2011, CMU-SUMMIT is a non-profit student organization. We aim to solve the toughest challenges in the world and make it a better place. We hold annual conference to discuss topics ranging from Artificial Intelligence to Biomedical Engineering. Our focus is to encourage innovative thinkings.
